MongoDB Crash Course

1 Day Workshop on Friday, September 30, 2016 (9am to 4pm)
ADD TO CALENDAR...

Running MongoDB effectively requires knowledge and experience. This workshop will arm you with some of both. We will be learning and applying key MongoDB concepts and scenarios

such as:

REGISTER

  • Document manipulation: creating, querying, and modifying
  • Indexing and performance tuning
  • Creating replica sets, and understanding automatic failover scenarios
  • Key document and schema design guidelines for successful projects

The workshop will be include presentations, discussions, and extensive hands-on labs. Attendees should be comfortable with general database and/or development concepts. Attendees can expect to walk away with a solid understanding of MongoDB clusters, how to design and create them, performance and tuning.
