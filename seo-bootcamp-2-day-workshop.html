SEO Bootcamp 2-Day Workshop

SEO Bootcamp 2 day Workshop with Massimo Paolini and Alyson Harrold
ADD TO CALENDAR...

A business’ visibility is won in the trenches. Unlike Search Engine Marketing (which relies upon paid advertising aka expensive artillery), SEO is an organic and more targeted process that relies upon technical infrastructure and purposeful content. In this two-day workshop, participants will learn SEO strategy and how to apply it on the SERP (Search Engine Results Page) battlefield.

REGISTER

Drills: Fundamentals & Research (Day 1)

This is a full day of push-ups and pulling reports. Bootcampers will:

  • Learn what SEO is and isn’t as it applies to an online marketing strategy
  • Review SEO terminology, including the difference between White Hat & Grey Hat tactics, PageRank & pagerank
  • Peek at some of the 200+ factors used by search engines to rank websites
  • Target practice with in-depth keyword research & competitor spying
  • Match keyword research to online buying cycle

Combat: Optimization & Measurement (Day 2)

Kick your optimization into high gear with some hand-to-hand SEO combat with.

  • Learn best practices in structuring a website for SEO
  • Elements of optimizing content for SEO’s 2 visitor types
  • Metrics to monitor and improve SEO effectiveness
  • Hands-on target practice using your website’s SEO Resources & Tools
  • Introduction to links
  • How to build an SEO strategy
